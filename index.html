About Rakshak IIT Bombay

Rakshak IITB is the official team of IIT Bombay, consisting of over 40 students from various disciplines. Since 2015, we have been developing robust Unmanned Aerial Vehicles (UAVs) to support Search and Rescue Operations (SRO) during disasters. Our aircraft provide relief measures in disaster-stricken areas and can be adapted for surveys, mapping, and wildlife conservation. Our designs tackle these challenges cost-effectively without compromising human safety.

To date, we have developed five planes. Each year, we push our limits through innovation and teamwork. We are grateful to IIT Bombay for supporting us and providing a platform to showcase our talents internationally.
